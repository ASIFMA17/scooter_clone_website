VINTAGE, NEW CLASSICS & ELECTRIC SCOOTER CONVERSION SPECIALISTS

Retrospective Scooters has specialised in restoring and maintaining vintage Vespa and Lambretta scooters since 1999. We've sourced a range of new modern classic petrol engine scooters from Royal Alloy to compliment our vintage Vespa & Lambretta restorations and have developed our very own electric conversion kits for all vintage Vespa & Lambretta scooters.

SCOOTER RESTORATION & SERVICE

Quite simply vintage scooter restoration, maintenance, servicing and repairs is what we love to do. Founded in 1999 because of our love of classic Italian Vespa scooters, it's what we're famous for around the globe.

ELECTRIC CONVERSON

A revolution in classic scooters. We have developed the worlds first electric motor conversion kit for classic Vespa & Lambretta scooters. Authentic classic looks and electric power all in one scooter.
